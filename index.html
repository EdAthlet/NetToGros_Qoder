Irish Payroll FAQ

How do I calculate net pay in Ireland?

Enter your gross pay and the calculator applies PAYE, USC and PRSI for the selected period and year.

Can I reverse from net to gross?

Yes — choose Net→Gross to estimate the gross needed for a target take-home.

Does it support weekly, fortnightly and monthly?

Yes, all three pay frequencies are supported for 2024–2026 rates.

How do I calculate multiple salaries at once?

Click the "Salary Range" toggle below the salary input field to reveal the upper salary field. Enter your starting salary in the main field and the upper salary in the range field — the button will change to "Calculate Range". When clicked, the calculator produces 11 evenly spaced results between the two values (inclusive) and saves them all to the History section. For example, entering €1,000 and €1,500 as a Weekly salary calculates at €1,000, €1,050, €1,100 and so on up to €1,500. To return to a single calculation, simply clear the upper salary field or collapse the Salary Range section — the button will revert to "Calculate". To use a dedicated multiple salaries calculator click on the link https://nettogross-eire.com/batch/.
